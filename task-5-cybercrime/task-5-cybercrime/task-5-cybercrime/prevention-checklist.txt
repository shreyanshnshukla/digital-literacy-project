Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist

1. Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone.
2. Always check links before you click (especially from emails and texts).
3. Look at the URL of websites carefully (it should start with "https" and be spelled correctly).
4. Avoid doing banking or transacting with sensitive details on public Wi-Fi.
5. Use two-factor authentication (2FA) on critical accounts.
6. Don't reply to strange emails or calls that ask for personal info.
7. For UPI security: Check the receiver ID very carefully before sending money.
8. For UPI security: Don't approve any collect requests from unknown persons.
9. Report any cybercrime at https://cybercrime.gov.in
10. Call the helpline number 1930 in case of a financial fraud.
