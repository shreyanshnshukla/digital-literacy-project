Safeguarding Your Digital Presence – Prevention is Key

*   Share your OTPs, passwords or bank account details with absolutely no one.
*   Always check all the links before opening them in any e-mail, message, or web link you encounter online.
*   Check the website URL in the address bar – be sure the website address is written correctly and starts with 'https' and ends with '.in' for banking websites.
*   Never conduct your banking or financial transactions while using a public Wi-Fi.
*   Use two-factor authentication (2FA) for all your critical accounts.
*   Do not reply to suspicious e-mails or calls where they ask for your personal information.
*   UPI Security: Always ensure that the Receiver's ID displayed is correct before you proceed with the money transfer.
*   UPI Security: Never approve the "collect request" from any unknown person.
*   Reportcyber crime on https://cybercrime.gov.in
*   Call helpline number 1930 on the occurrence of any financial fraud.
